Thesis Supervision

Dr. Li has supervised a total of 7 Ph.D. dissertations and 30 Master's theses, all conducted at CINVESTAV-IPN, Mexico.

Students supervised by Dr. Li have worked on topics such as Machine Learning, Petri Nets, Social Network Analysis, Robotics, Rehabilitation Systems, Quantum Support Vector Machines and Sustainable Energy Forecasting.
