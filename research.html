Research & Funding

Research lines, funded projects and active areas that can serve as starting points for Master's and Ph.D. thesis work in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Robotics and Sustainable Systems.

Xiaoou Li has been the Principal Investigator (PI) or Co-PI of 11 research projects and academic activities funded by the national science foundations of Mexico and China. Additionally, she has participated in 4 basic theoretical research and industrial projects.

Research Interests & Thesis Areas

These research lines can serve as starting points for Master's and Ph.D. thesis projects.

  • Advanced Machine Learning and Deep Neural Architectures
  • Autonomous Systems and Intelligent Control for Ecological Sustainability
  • Intelligent Robotics and Adaptive Human-Robot Interaction
  • Knowledge Representation, Pattern Recognition, and AI Education
