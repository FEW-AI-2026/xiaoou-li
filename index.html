Research Overview

Dr. Xiaoou Li leads the Data Science Laboratory at Cinvestav, where students work on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Petri Nets, Robotics and Sustainable Systems.

Her research combines strong theoretical foundations with real-world applications in energy, health, robotics, cyber-physical systems and data-driven decision making.

Motivated Master's and Ph.D. students are welcome to explore thesis opportunities related to intelligent systems and applied AI.
